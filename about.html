About Me

I’m Brennan Reilly — a sound designer, audio engineer, and production manager.

I’ve spent the last several years touring with family shows, mixing musical theatre and live bands, managing logistics, and running crews. I’ve worked in theaters, arenas, fields, schools, and convention centers — whatever the job calls for.

My approach is practical and people-focused. I build systems that make sense, solve problems before they slow things down, and keep shows running clean and on time.

I’m available for freelance work in sound design, engineering, and live events.
